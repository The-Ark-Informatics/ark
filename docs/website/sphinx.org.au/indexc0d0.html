Welcome to SPHINX - the School of Population Health INformatiX website.

SPHINX is situated in the Centre for Epidemiology and Biostatistics in the Melbourne School of Population and Global Health at The University of Melbourne.

SPHINX combines hardware and software capable of enabling high quality management, tracking, and analysis of data from studies, including those involving the collection and analyses of biospecimens, combined with the intellectual input of experts in study design, conduct and analysis.

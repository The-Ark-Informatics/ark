Edit Consent Details

 

  1. Click on the Patient tab.

  2. Enter any available patient identifiers into the search fields and click Search. The results of your search will be displayed on the View Patient screen.

  3. Click on the Consent link across the top of the screen. You will be taken to the Patient Consent screen where an overall list of Studies Consented to will be displayed.

  4. Click on the Delete button to the study you wish to edit. A pop up will appear asking you “are you sure you want to delete this record?”

  5. Click on the OK button.

  6. Click on the Study drop down box, and highlight the relevant study.

  7. Click on the Approved drop down box and choose Yes.

  8. Enter details into the required field Approved Date.

  9. Enter the new information into the required fields.

  10. Click on the Save Consent button. The consent details will be saved and can be viewed on a separate line.

  11. To attach a signed consent form, click on the Add link next to the newly created consent record. You will be taken to the Add Consent File screen.

  12. Click on the Browse button. You will be taken to the relevant file directory.

  13. Select the relevant consent form and click on the Open button. The directory path will be recorded in the Select Signed Consent Form field.

  14. Click on the Save File button. You will be taken back to the Patient Consent screen.

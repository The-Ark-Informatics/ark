Edit Patient Details

 

  1. Click on the Patient tab.

  2. Enter any available patient identifiers into the search fields and click Search. The results of your search will be displayed.

  3. Click on the Patient Details under any of the Search Results categories. You will be taken to the View Patient screen were the patients details are displayed.

  4. Edit the fields that you want to change.

  5. Click on the Save button at the bottom of the View Patient screen. Details will be saved.
